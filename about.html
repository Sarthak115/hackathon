Features

The features of this website include using the farmer's location data to provide tailored seed recommendations based on the terrain status, water availability, and weather conditions. Farmers can select fertilizers based on the specific growth stage of their crops and the optimal harvesting time. Additionally, we offer guidance on two types of advanced irrigation systems: the drip system and the sprinkler system. These systems are designed to minimize water wastage while ensuring crops receive the right amount of water for healthy growth.

Purpose

This website is designed to provide farmers with access to high-quality seeds, fertilizers, and irrigation resources to ensure a better harvest. By offering essential agricultural inputs, we aim to support farmers in achieving higher yields and improving the overall quality of their crops.
